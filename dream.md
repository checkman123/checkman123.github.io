## Into the Endless Dream (Unity)

**This is an Ongoing Project**

**Project description:** This is an endless 2D rogue-like game with a theme of Native American and Aztec.
The player will gather items and equipments to fight each level boss and its minion to go to the next level.
As time goes on, the amount of monsters and elite monsters will increase untl the player dies.

**Skills**
- UI/UX Design
- Designed and Animated 2D character models
- Real time interaction
